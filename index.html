Donate for Flood at Noakhali, Bangladesh

Noakhali has been severely affected by prolonged flooding since mid-August 2024, with over 90% of its population impacted. Around 1.3 million people have been stranded, with many displaced from their homes, seeking shelter in schools and other institutions. The floods have caused significant damage to infrastructure, homes, and farmlands. Water drainage issues have worsened due to encroachments on canals and poor maintenance, leading to stagnation of floodwaters. Recovery efforts have been slow, and many are struggling to meet basic needs like food and clean water
